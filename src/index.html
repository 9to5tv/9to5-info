9to5 : Works in Progress is a month long exhibition showcasing projects in Atlanta that are influenced by the online community through collaborative tools. Participants will work at the CUBE and debut their projects via our live stream.

Artists, creators, directors, and anyone that wants to explore new ways of using live streaming as a way to collaborate with the world should apply to our Open Call!

Apply to Open Call

9to5 will feature artists and creators that use live streaming with participation from the online audience to create a collaborative outcome. We are looking for projects that want to explore new ways of using live streaming as a way to connect and collaborate with the world. From data-vis to vr, from workshops to idle games, from storytelling to interactive music, from dance to hackathons.

9to5 is equal parts: Artist Residency, Gallery, Classroom, & Television Network

We’ll stream artists creating their works, site specific multi media projects, social activism panels, new media workshops, panel shows, podcasts, visual shows, and more. All of these segments will be divided into channels and programming blocks throughout our month long exhibition.

We’ll host idle web based projects on our website gallery and display real life site specific projects installations in our INSIDE Gallery, all connected to the web ready for participation from the world.
