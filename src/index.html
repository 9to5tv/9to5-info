9to5
Sep 8 – Oct 6 2017
APPLY

9to5 : Works in Progress is a month long exhibition showcasing projects in Atlanta that are influenced by the online community through collaborative tools. Participants will work at the CUBE and debut their projects via our live stream.

Artists, creators, directors, and anyone that wants to explore new ways of using live streaming as a way to collaborate with the world should apply to our Open Call!
