Partners

The 9to5 Beta was one of five forward-thinking finalists for Field Experiment, an annual grant competition sponsored by The Hambidge Center for the Creative Arts and The Goat Farm. Our winning week featured five full nights of distinct programming, with each night using our collaborative livestream platform differently. Over the last year our team has been working to produce the second iteration of this concept. This copy should talk about the types of partnership and some evocative text that highlights the fact that we are still looking for active partnerships in the form financial backers, technology support, development, and in kind donations of all kinds.
