Participating Artists

DRAWN / SUPERBRIGHT & Art404

superbright.me Head to head VR Drawing Tool with one node in Atlanta and the other at TV 404 in New York. Battles involving both nodes will be streamed over the course of the month, along with solo node "Pictionary" where the audience tries to guess the subject being drawn.

Brannon Dorsey / Holy Pager

brannondorsey.com Pagers are actually capable of receiving all pages that come through their network. However, only the pages directed at you are the ones you receive. Brannon hacked three pagers to display and print every page that is sent on their network. No digital component.

Kevin Siwoff / Basix Lockr

kevinsiwoff.com Satirical ecommerce perfomarce art / new media piece. Users order "goods" (really just paper mache-esque interpretations of what they ordered). Eventually those projects are delivered to The Cube, unboxed (which will be main livestream events), and placed on livestream pedestals.

Andrew Venell / Supercruft Induction Terminal

andrewvenell.com An interactive station in Atlanta with two streams of edited found video footage that can be combined into infinite abstract compositions by the Cube user. The user's creation will be livestreamed in real time on a microsite.
